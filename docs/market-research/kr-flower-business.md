# KR Flower Business Snapshot

## Market and Operating Context
- Strong event-driven demand: funerals, openings, anniversaries, graduation, Parents' Day.
- High expectation for same-day delivery and strict promised-time delivery windows.
- Offline shop + online channel mixed operations are common.

## Channel / Messaging / Order Intake
- Naver SmartStore and Cafe24 are common commerce bases.
- KakaoTalk and phone calls are still major custom-order channels.
- Many stores still re-enter messenger orders manually into POS/ERP.

## Payment / Settlement / Tax Notes
- Card + bank transfer coexist, business-account transfer still frequent for B2B.
- VAT handling and channel fee visibility are key pain points for owners.
- Delivery-agent fee and platform commission often split across multiple sheets.

## Logistics and Production Reality
- Delivery route batching and timing is critical during peak hours.
- Message card / ribbon print quality and speed directly affect customer satisfaction.
- Out-of-stock confusion often comes from delayed inventory updates.

## Country-Localized Feature Candidates
- KakaoTalk order parsing (high priority).
- Naver/Cafe24 real-time sync and reconciliation.
- Time-slot aware delivery dispatch + driver fee logic.
- Korean-style document templates (estimate, tax, settlement summaries).

## Suggested Priority (KR)
- P0: order intake automation, channel sync, print bridge.
- P1: settlement transparency and per-order margin analysis.
- P2: seasonal demand forecasting and advanced CRM segmentation.
