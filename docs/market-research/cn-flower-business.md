# CN Flower Business Snapshot

## Market and Operating Context
- Large digital-commerce market with app-first customer behavior.
- Strong festival and gifting cycles with high peak traffic bursts.
- High competition on delivery speed and storefront responsiveness.

## Channel / Messaging / Order Intake
- WeChat (official account / mini-program / private chat) is critical.
- Local delivery platforms and marketplace channels can generate fragmented order streams.
- Unified order view is necessary to prevent missed fulfillment.

## Payment / Settlement / Tax Notes
- Mobile wallet payment patterns dominate consumer flow.
- Platform commissions and promotion subsidies can complicate true margin tracking.
- Shop owners need transparent per-order profit decomposition.

## Logistics and Production Reality
- Instant/near-instant fulfillment expectations in major cities.
- Message consistency and print quality are still important for premium gifts.
- SKU freshness and replacement handling are operationally sensitive.

## Country-Localized Feature Candidates
- WeChat-oriented order ingestion and customer messaging templates.
- Wallet/platform fee-aware settlement engine.
- Fast-ops dispatch board for city delivery cadence.
- Chinese-language templates and local date/number formatting.

## Suggested Priority (CN)
- P0: multi-channel order unification + fast dispatch.
- P1: fee/subsidy-aware settlement.
- P2: advanced customer segmentation and campaign automation.
