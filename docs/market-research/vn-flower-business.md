# VN Flower Business Snapshot

## Market and Operating Context
- Fast-growing urban flower delivery demand (HCMC, Hanoi).
- Social-commerce style ordering is very common.
- Price sensitivity + speed expectation both high for daily gift orders.

## Channel / Messaging / Order Intake
- Facebook page/inbox, Zalo, and direct chat-based ordering are dominant in many shops.
- Marketplace and website orders often coexist with chat orders.
- Manual copying from chat to order sheet is a recurring bottleneck.

## Payment / Settlement / Tax Notes
- Bank transfer and COD-like workflows are common in small businesses.
- Confirmation screenshot-based payment checks still happen in practice.
- Multi-channel fee tracking is often weak.

## Logistics and Production Reality
- Same-day and short-notice delivery demand is high.
- Driver matching and ETA communication are operationally important.
- Product substitutions due to fresh stock variance are frequent.

## Country-Localized Feature Candidates
- Zalo/Facebook chat order parsing and template extraction.
- Transfer confirmation workflow and lightweight payment reconciliation.
- Fast dispatch board with status updates for customer chat replies.
- Vietnamese document and message templates.

## Suggested Priority (VN)
- P0: chat-order automation + quick dispatch workflow.
- P1: payment confirmation and channel-level settlement.
- P2: substitution rules and recommendation automation.
