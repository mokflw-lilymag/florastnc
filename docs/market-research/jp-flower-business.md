# JP Flower Business Snapshot

## Market and Operating Context
- Quality consistency, punctual delivery, and polite communication are top expectations.
- Corporate gifting and ceremonial orders can require strict format and timing.
- Reliability and process traceability are valued over aggressive automation messaging.

## Channel / Messaging / Order Intake
- LINE and web/e-commerce channels are common for customer contact and ordering.
- Manual handoff from inquiry to confirmed production order still exists.
- Structured confirmation workflow is preferred for error prevention.

## Payment / Settlement / Tax Notes
- Card, transfer, and invoice-style business payments can coexist.
- Precise fee/tax breakdown and clean reporting are important.
- Monthly closure quality is often prioritized by management.

## Logistics and Production Reality
- Narrow delivery windows and order promise discipline are crucial.
- Premium packaging/ribbon/message detail quality matters for brand trust.
- Work instructions should be clear and standardized for staff handover.

## Country-Localized Feature Candidates
- LINE-centered inquiry-to-order conversion flow.
- High-precision print layout presets and QA checklist.
- Detailed order timeline/audit trail per operation step.
- Japanese business document templates.

## Suggested Priority (JP)
- P0: standardized order confirmation + production handoff.
- P1: print QA/preset management + timeline traceability.
- P2: predictive staffing/load planning for peak periods.
