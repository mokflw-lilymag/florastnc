# ES Flower Business Snapshot

## Market and Operating Context
- Mix of local neighborhood florists and online delivery operators.
- Event and holiday seasonality strongly influences demand (weddings, funerals, local festivals).
- Fast communication and clear delivery promises are key for repeat customers.

## Channel / Messaging / Order Intake
- WhatsApp Business is highly practical for order conversations.
- Website/e-commerce and social channels may run in parallel with chat sales.
- Duplicate entry from chat to management sheet is a frequent inefficiency.

## Payment / Settlement / Tax Notes
- Card + transfer + cash-related edge cases may coexist by business type.
- VAT and channel fee visibility still affects margin confidence.
- Owners need simple, explainable profitability view by order/channel.

## Logistics and Production Reality
- Last-mile coordination quality impacts customer reviews significantly.
- Substitution handling and customer approval flow are operationally important.
- Label/card/ribbon print consistency remains a differentiator for premium segment.

## Country-Localized Feature Candidates
- WhatsApp-first order capture and template replies.
- Channel-wise margin visibility with VAT-aware settlement outputs.
- Delivery promise board with route/status updates.
- Spanish-language templates and localized legal/report wording.

## Suggested Priority (ES)
- P0: WhatsApp order automation + dispatch board.
- P1: VAT/channel-fee-aware settlement reporting.
- P2: campaign automation and loyalty segmentation.
